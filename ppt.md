SLIDE 5: LOCAL CONTEXT – AVRC REGION IX

Area Vocational Rehabilitation Center Region IX
• Location: Zamboanga City, Philippines
• Active PWD Clients: 45
• Staff Members: 38
• Services: Vocational training, counseling, skills development

Current Challenges Observed:
• Manual documentation systems
• Fragmented record-keeping
• Limited progress tracking mechanisms
• No centralized client database

SLIDE 6: BEHAVIORAL CHALLENGES IN VOCATIONAL TRAINING

Key Behavioral Challenges Identified:
• Emotional Dysregulation
• Impulsivity
• Absenteeism
• Noncompliance

Impact Pathway:
Behavioral Challenges → Reduced Training Participation → Lower Productivity → Decreased Employment Readiness → Poor Job Outcomes

SLIDE 7: PROBLEM STATEMENT

Core Problem:
PWDs at AVRC Region IX face persistent challenges in achieving successful employment outcomes due to the absence of an integrated system that monitors development across critical stages.

Root Causes:
• Manual/fragmented documentation
• No centralized monitoring platform
• Limited visibility of individual progress
• Delayed access to client information
• Inconsistent intervention documentation

Consequences:
• Inefficient data retrieval
• Gaps in timely support
• Reduced client motivation
• Lower employment success rates

SLIDE 8: RESEARCH QUESTIONS
How does the current manual/fragmented system affect monitoring of PWD client progress, productivity, and employment readiness?

What are the limitations of existing tools in tracking behavioral development, training performance, and job placement outcomes?

To what extent can InkluTrack improve efficiency, accuracy, and effectiveness of monitoring PWD development?

How effective is the system in providing AI-based recommendations to support decision-making?

What is the level of usability, accessibility, and user satisfaction among intended users?

SLIDE 10: GENERAL OBJECTIVE

To design and develop an AI-enabled, lifecycle-based smart application that monitors, analyzes, and enhances the productivity development of persons with disabilities from enrollment to job placement at AVRC Region IX, Zamboanga City.

Three Core Functions:
• Monitor – Continuous tracking across all lifecycle stages
• Analyze – AI-driven insights and productivity evaluation
• Enhance – Recommendations that improve outcomes
